GLOWZONE COVID-19 VACCINATION POLICY & GLOWZONE SAFETY STATEMENT

UPDATED COVID POLICY: In the event that a party is cancelled within 7 days due to the birthday child or Legal Guardian testing positive for Covid - The party can be rescheduled within a 3 month period from the date of the original booking. In the event of a Government mandated Lockdown any party booked during the lockdown period will be refunded or rescheduled within a 12 month period.

The health and well being of our staff, guests, their families and all patrons entering our venue is our number one priority.

As such GlowZone is governed by strict protocols where we have a COVID-19 Safe Plan in place (implemented safe distancing protocols with density levels, hand sanitise stations and strict infection control measures) as per government guidelines.

To enter The GlowZone, all patrons 16 years and over must have received both COVID-19 vaccinations and will need to show proof of their vaccination status at our point of entry either via:

Services Victoria App
COVID-19 Digital Certificate printed or via their phone wallet

Patrons advising of medical exemption must show proof of this exemption before entry is granted.

GlowZone will continue to update our COVID-19 policies in line with the Victorian government’s set guidelines and advice issued by the Department of Health and Human Services Victoria.

GlowZone staff deserve the right to refuse entry to patrons who refuse to abide by our strict health and safety entry requirements.

We thank you all for your understanding.

GlowZone looks forward to working in partnership with everyone to ensure GlowZone’s safety policies are adhered to and we continue to be one of Australia’s leading party venues.
