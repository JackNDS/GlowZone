Frequently Asked Questions

IS THERE A MINIMUM NUMBER OF GUESTS FOR A PARTY BOOKING?

All of our 90 minute packages including the mega combination package have a minimum of 12. All of our deluxe and mega combination deluxe packages have a minimum of 16.

IS A DEPOSIT REQUIRED WHEN BOOKING A PARTY?

A $324.00 deposit is required when booking your party; the final balance must be paid by the Wednesday prior to your party. This deposit is non-refundable.

WHAT IS THE YOUNGEST AGE YOU PROVIDE PARTIES FOR?

Unfortunately we are unable to offer glow in the dark experiences to children under the age of 5. Younger siblings and friends are welcome to attend parties but they are unable to enter our sports arena or take part in activities.

WHAT DOES THE PARTY HOST DO?

The party host will entertain the kids during the party and organises all the sports/activities for the party guests. They will also split children into teams and umpire the sports ensuring all children enjoy the experience regardless of ability or expertise.

CAN WE BRING OUR OWN FOOD?

Yes. All GlowZone Parties are self catered. You have total control over what your party guests eat and food can be prepared using our kitchenette facilities.

DO WE NEED TO ARRIVE EARLIER THAN OUR BOOKING TIME?

We request that you arrive 15 minutes prior to your party start time, and guests arrive 5 minutes prior to ensure that your party experience begins on time and that none of your guests miss out on any of the fun! Your party will commence on the hour.

WHAT IF I HAVE MORE THAN 16 GUESTS?

We are required to have 2 party hosts for all parties of 16 or more participants. Please advise us ASAP should your numbers exceed 16 to allow us to roster extra staff. Please note: A fee of $80.00 applies for an additional party host, for 16 to 29 people. An additional charge of $160 applies to numbers of 30 or more people.

DO YOU HAVE KIOSK FACILITIES?

We offer a communal kiosk space upstairs. Available are tea and coffee and assorted drinks, chips etc for purchase. This space is available for all parties, both upstairs and downstairs.
